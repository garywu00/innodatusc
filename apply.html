Membership FAQ

Do I need experience to apply?

Although we're looking for members with past experience in a specific discipline, we're always looking for the potential growth in members interested in growing in their creative field. However, if you have any work that can show us your creative abilities, we'd love to see that in your application.

What are some of the benefits of joining Innovative Design?

By being part of Innovative Design, you're part of a community of creatives looking to support the greater design community at USC. You'll have access to our workshops, speaker events, and gain an alumni network spanning from USC to UC Berkeley.
