Innovative Design at USC started because of a Facebook post. A few friends from Berkeley recently updated their profile photos to the latest InnoD recruitment theme, and it caught the attention of InnoD at USC’s design students. From there, an idea sparked into a conversation, and that conversation was brought to life virtually in Summer 2020. The rest is history...
