Services

For Clients

Service applications open a few weeks before the start of each school semester. Our next application will open August 2021.

Next steps after submitting your request

  • Step 1

    Our team will contact you by email if we think you are a good fit.

  • Step 2

    Hop on a call with us to discuss and confirm your requested work.

  • Step 3

    You will be matched with one of our teams to complete your request by the end of the semester!

Clients FAQ

Do I qualify to request a service?

If you are a registered student organization at USC, non-profit or startup, you meet the qualifications to request a service.

Does Innovative Design charge money?

No! We don’t charge our clients. All of our work is pro-bono. You can support our organization through our fundraisers.

How do I know if my request is incompatible?

Some projects may be too large or small for our teams to complete by the end of the semester.
